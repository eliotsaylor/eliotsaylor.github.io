Legal Guides

Curated guides prepared by Law Library staff on common legal topics. Each guide brings together relevant laws, court forms, local resources, and research tools in one place to help you get started.

Note: These guides are provided for informational purposes to help you find legal resources. Law Library staff cannot provide legal advice. If you need legal advice, consider consulting with an attorney or contacting a legal aid organization. Looking for links to courts, statutes, and government websites? Visit Legal Resources.

Landlord-Tenant Housing

A guide to the residential eviction process in California, landlord-tenant laws at the state and local level, court forms, e-filing tools, and legal publications. Includes resources for both tenants and landlords, local ordinances for Santa Barbara County cities, and information about free legal assistance.
